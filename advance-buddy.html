Advanced Buddy for Binusian 2027

Advanced Buddy is a collaborative program between the Student Development Center and Marketing Unit of BINUS @Malang designed to introduce prospective students to university life at BINUS while establishing a communication bridge between BINUS @Malang campus and future students.

This three-month program (February 2023 to May 2023) encompassed a variety of activities, including campus introductions, "What You Wanna Be" sharing sessions, introductions to each department's academic systems, and a final project featuring creative videos on Instagram and TikTok about "Why You Choose BINUS @Malang."

As an Advanced Buddy, I had the opportunity to mentor prospective Binusian 2027 students, guiding them through their transition from high school to university life, answering their questions about majors and campus life, and helping them build connections within the BINUS community before they officially enrolled.
