CerdasAps Bahasa Isyarat

CerdasAps Bahasa Isyarat is a comprehensive web-based sign language learning application designed to bridge communication gaps between hearing and deaf communities. The platform features an extensive library of sign language videos and photos that enable interactive learning experiences.

The application provides step-by-step tutorials through high-quality videos demonstrating proper hand movements and expressions for various words and phrases. Users can track their progress, practice with interactive exercises, and receive feedback on their signing techniques through the integrated learning system.

Key features include categorized learning modules, search functionality for specific signs, slow-motion video playback for detailed movement study, and a community forum for users to share experiences and ask questions.
