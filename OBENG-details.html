OBENG - Online Bengkel Service Platform

OBENG (Online Bengkel) is a comprehensive web platform designed to connect vehicle owners with automotive repair shops. This project addresses the challenge of finding reliable automotive services by creating a streamlined booking system and service marketplace.

The platform features user authentication, service booking, repair shop profiles, service listings, and payment integration. The responsive design ensures accessibility across devices, making vehicle maintenance more convenient for all users.
